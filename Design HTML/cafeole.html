Brand Guidelines - Cafe Olé

Softwares: Illustrator - InDesign

Project Brief:

Create brand guidelines with a style guide for a special event or initiative. This means you will choose an existing brand and redesign or adapt existing guidelines for a one-off event. For example, the Olympics are a brand, and the 2024 Paris Olympics were a special event.

The purpose of a style guide is to protect the visual integrity of a brand. This is achieved when a designer creates an expansive and cohesive family of graphic brand-enhancing elements that other designers will use to make stuff - anything from water bottles to sneakers to bedsheets.

Create at least six pages with ready-to-use logos, graphics, color palettes, patterns, and text or slogans. Include some examples of products and how your graphics might be used.

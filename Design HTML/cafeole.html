Brand Guidelines - Cafe Olé

Softwares: Illustrator - InDesign

Project Brief:

The brand guidelines presented below where developed for an imagined special event based on 'Cafe Ole' an underdeveloped character from the Strawberry Shortcake franchise. In this instance, I developed a coffee themed pop-up based on her character design and little information that was known of her character.

The purpose of a style guide is to protect the visual integrity of a brand and as such I remained loyal to a lot of the design conventions from Strawberry Shortcake. I worked to create an expansive and cohesive family of graphic elements that can be easily turned into physical products or applied to other graphic materials.

The result was six pages with ready-to-use logos, graphics, color palettes, and patterns. Along with some simple mockups to illustrate how the graphics might be used.

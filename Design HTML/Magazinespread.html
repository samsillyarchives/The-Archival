5 page spread - Music Magazine Publication

Softwares: InDesign

Project Brief:

Create a magazine cover and two double spread layouts for a music publication. This comes out to a total of five pages. create a cover and spreads for an existing publication, such as Rolling Stone, Decibel, Tom Tom, XXL, or any other magazine that's primarily about music.

The pages should be 8 1/2 x 11, at 300 dpi, or the exact measurements of the actual magazine for which you are going to design. Your spreads should include at least some but not necessarily all of the following elements: headlines, decks (or subheads), lead-ins, body, captions, a byline, photo credits, page numbers, publication title, dates, and pull quotes. For body type, you can use Lorem Ipsum, or text from Wikipedia or Allmusic.com, or Chat GPT.
